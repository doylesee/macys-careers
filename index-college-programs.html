Let's build your career.

Supporting a fortune 500 corporation, with over 600 sites as well as one of the nation's largest online retail operations means that you're in for an unmatched experience. You'll be facing new challenges daily, putting to use every bit of knowledge you have as well as learning at a rapid pace. At Macy's Technology you'll be supported by a world-class leadership team, so no challenge is too daunting.

Growth

From day one, you’ll not only make a contribution, you’ll gain an education. Through our Executive Development Program (EDP), we treat your career as an investment, preparing you to lead throughout every step in your career.

Be Connected

Macy's Technology is home to several employee resource groups (ERGs), each designed to activate your interests and connect you with like-minded individuals. Support everything from health and wellness initiatives to millennial development and increasing the awareness and acceptance of the LGBTQ community. Learn more about our Employee resource groups.

Innovation

Through the Macy’s Technology Executive Development Program, you’ll have the opportunity to dig into leading-edge interactive solutions. When you do, you’ll find an integrated, deliberate, strategic mix of wireless, Internet, client/server and midrange technologies. As the leader in retail technology we’re always looking for ways to push boundaries and move into unknown spaces, as a member of our EDP program you will be encouraged to think outside of the box to find newer and better ways to help our customers.
