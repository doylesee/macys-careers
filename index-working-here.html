We're problem solvers.

Our passion for teamwork and creative thinking push forward innovative ideas and help bring them to life. Simply put, our culture defines our brand.

We created the curve in retail technology, and now it’s our job to stay ahead of it. Our mission is simple: stay driven and continue to take out-of-the-box thinking and set the pace in retail technology.

Social Responsibility.

We think about our impact on the environment and the people inhabiting it. That’s why we’re proud to be named one of Newsweek Magazine’s 500 Greenest Companies in America.

As a Fortune 200 company, we understand we have power to make a positive impact. Head over to macysgreenliving.com to see all things Macy’s and sustainability. Check out our Report on Social Responsibility to learn more.

Community Involvement.

We all want to do more for our community. But let’s be honest—so often work and life gets in the way of helping others. At Macy’s, you don’t have to choose one over the other. Opportunities to get involved with your community are everywhere you look. From funding for arts and education, HIV/AIDS research and awareness and women’s issues to supporting associate giving and volunteerism—we know these issues are important and want to make a positve impact.

Diversity & Inclusion.

Our different backgrounds and unique perspectives allow us to challenge and inspire each other; it’s how we learn and grow. Women represent more than 75% of the Macy’s, Inc. workforce and approximately 64% of management-level executives. Ethnic minorities represent more than 59% of our associate team and 36% of our management team. We were recognized as a best place to work for LGBT equality by the Human Rights Campaign’s Corporate Equality Index. Hispanic Business ranked us in the top 10 of their Diversity Elite list for our commitment to Hispanic hiring, promotion, marketing and philanthropy.
