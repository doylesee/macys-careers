Three Hubs | One Macy's Technology

Our Macy’s Technology locations span the country, and each location provides a unique mix of technology and culture. Whether you’re looking for a high-energy place to mix work and social life or a quiet place to build a career and family, Macy’s Technology locations offers choices, each with something special to showcase.

San Francisco, California

San Francisco, a true American melting pot and cultural hub is one of today’s foremost destinations for forward thinkers. Living here brings you access to resources unmatched—from cuisine to climate, athletics to adventure its all at your fingertips.

Located in the heart of downtown's San Francisco's SoMa district is Macy’s Technology. Boasting an award-winning space which is designed to be open and collaborative, welcoming exchange of ideas and inspirational views to keep your mind active.

It’s a convergence of the best and brightest engineers, developers, brand experts and analysts—all collaborating in an environment that’s fast-paced yet friendly, equal parts tech-savvy and talented.

Atlanta, Georgia

Atlanta is a big city with a big heart. From major league sports teams to world-class theater, it’s a family-friendly city with great weather and welcoming neighbors. Just a short ride to the Northeast, our Johns Creek technology office is a buzzing environment full of diverse technical professionals changing the way retail operates in the 21st century.

Johns Creek is a culturally diverse city boasting nationally ranked schools and over 300 acres of parks and recreation. Voted the Best City in Georgia to Raise a Family, it’s an ideal location for our technologists and their loved ones.

Explore opportunities

Cleveland, Ohio

Nestled in the Midwest, Cleveland's rebirth is making national headlines. The city has a vibrant arts, music and culinary scene that's home to six James Beard award-wining chef-inspired restaurants. Our Lorain, OH offices are just miles off the shore of Lake Erie and a quick commute away from the hustling culture of downtown.

Cleveland is a great city that's quickly luring some of America's best and brightest technologists away from Silicon Valley, Austin and other cities. Join our inspired team and help us push forward the digitization of retail.
